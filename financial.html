Frequently Asked Questions

Quick answers to common questions.

How do I reset my online banking password?

Click "Forgot Password" on the login page and follow the verification steps. You'll receive a reset link via email or SMS.

What is the daily ATM withdrawal limit?

The standard daily ATM withdrawal limit is $500. Premium members enjoy limits up to $1,000. Contact us to request an increase.

How do I dispute a transaction?

Open a support case or call us at 1-800-MERIDIAN. Disputes must be filed within 60 days of the transaction date.

How long do wire transfers take?

Domestic wire transfers typically complete same-day. International transfers take 1-5 business days depending on the destination.
