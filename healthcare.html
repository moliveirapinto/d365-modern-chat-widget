Frequently Asked Questions

Quick answers to common patient questions.

How do I access my medical records?

Log into the Patient Portal with your credentials. If you need assistance with portal access, contact our Health Information department at (555) 123-4567.

What insurance plans do you accept?

We accept most major insurance plans including Medicare, Medicaid, Blue Cross, Aetna, UnitedHealthcare, and Cigna. Contact billing for specific coverage questions.

How do I request a prescription refill?

You can request refills through the Patient Portal, by calling your pharmacy, or by contacting your physician's office directly during business hours.

What are the visiting hours?

General visiting hours are 10:00 AM - 8:00 PM daily. ICU and specialty units may have different hours. Please check with the nursing station upon arrival.
