Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to common questions about shopping with us

How long does shipping take?
Standard shipping typically takes 5-7 business days within the continental US. Express shipping (2-3 business days) and overnight options are available at checkout. International shipping times vary by destination, usually 7-14 business days.
What is your return policy?
We offer a 30-day return policy for unworn items with original tags attached. Simply initiate a return through your account or contact our support team. Refunds are processed within 5-7 business days after we receive the item.
Do you offer international shipping?
Yes! We ship to over 50 countries worldwide. Shipping costs and delivery times vary by destination. All duties and taxes are calculated at checkout for a hassle-free delivery experience.
How do I track my order?
Once your order ships, you'll receive an email with a tracking number. You can also track your order by logging into your account and viewing your order history. Real-time updates are provided throughout the delivery process.
What payment methods do you accept?
We accept all major credit cards (Visa, Mastercard, American Express, Discover), PayPal, Apple Pay, Google Pay, and Shop Pay. All transactions are secured with 256-bit SSL encryption.
How do I find my size?
Each product page includes a detailed size guide with measurements in both inches and centimeters. If you're between sizes, we recommend sizing up for a more relaxed fit or contacting our support team for personalized recommendations.
