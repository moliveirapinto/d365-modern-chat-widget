Frequently Asked Questions

How do I access the student portal?

Visit portal.crestwood.edu and sign in with your Crestwood ID (e.g., jdoe24) and password. If you've forgotten your credentials, use the "Forgot Password" link or contact the IT Help Desk.

When is the add/drop deadline?

The add/drop deadline for the current semester is typically the end of the second week of classes. Check the Academic Calendar on the Registrar's page for exact dates.

How do I apply for on-campus housing?

Log into the student portal and navigate to Housing & Residential Life. Complete the housing application and preference form by the spring deadline. Room assignments are released in June.

What mental health resources are available?

The Crestwood Counseling Center offers free confidential sessions for all enrolled students. Walk-in hours are Monday-Friday 9 AM - 4 PM. For after-hours support, call the 24/7 crisis line at (555) 123-HELP.

How do I request an official transcript?

Official transcripts can be ordered through the student portal under Registrar > Transcripts. Electronic transcripts are delivered within 1-2 business days. Physical copies take 5-7 business days.
