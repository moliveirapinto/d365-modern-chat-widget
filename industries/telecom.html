Frequently Asked Questions

How do I check if 5G is available in my area?

Visit our Coverage page and enter your address to see available network types in your area. You can also download the NovaTel app for real-time coverage maps and speed tests.

Can I keep my current phone number when switching?

Yes! We make it easy to transfer your existing number. During activation, simply choose "Keep my number" and we'll handle the rest. The process typically takes 15-30 minutes.

What happens when I use all my high-speed data?

On Essential and Premium plans, your speeds may be temporarily reduced during network congestion after using your high-speed data allotment. Ultimate plan customers enjoy unlimited premium data with no slowdowns.

Do I need a new phone to use 5G?

Yes, you'll need a 5G-capable device to access our 5G network. Most smartphones released after 2021 support 5G. Check our device compatibility tool on the Shop page or bring your phone to a store for a free check.

How do I set up mobile hotspot?

Go to your phone's Settings → Network → Hotspot and toggle it on. Your hotspot data allowance depends on your plan. You can track usage in the NovaTel app.
