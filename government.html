Frequently Asked Questions

Quick answers to common citizen questions.

How do I pay my utility bill?

You can pay online through our portal, by phone at (555) 123-4567, by mail, or in person at City Hall during business hours.

How do I report a pothole?

Use our online "Report an Issue" form, call 311, or submit a request through the Citizen Support Center.

When is bulk trash pickup?

Bulk trash pickup is scheduled monthly. Check the Sanitation calendar or call for your neighborhood's schedule.

How do I get a building permit?

Building permits can be applied for online or in person at the Planning Department. Most permits require 5-10 business days.
